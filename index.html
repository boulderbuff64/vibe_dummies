What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding is the art of building software through conversation. Instead of memorizing programming languages, you learn to communicate your vision to AI assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, and GitHub Copilot. It's not just about prompts - it's about vibing with your AI partner until the code feels right.

In this book, you'll discover:

  • Prompt Engineering Mastery - Learn the psychology of AI assistants and how to craft prompts that generate production-ready code
  • The VIBE Framework - Vision, Iterate, Build, Evaluate - a proven methodology for AI-assisted development
  • Error Whispering - Turn cryptic error messages into opportunities for AI debugging
  • Context Architecture - Structure your conversations for maximum AI comprehension
  • Multi-Agent Workflows - Coordinate multiple AI assistants for complex projects
  • The Art of Accepting "Good Enough" - Ship features without perfectionism paralysis

Entrepreneurs who want to build without hiring developers. Developers who want to 10x their productivity. Product managers who are tired of waiting for engineering. Anyone who has ever thought, "I have a great app idea but I can't code."

About the Author

Ritu Java

Ritu Java is a tech entrepreneur and the founder of three successful startups built entirely through vibe coding. After spending 15 years as a traditional software engineer, she discovered that describing problems to AI was more effective than solving them herself.
